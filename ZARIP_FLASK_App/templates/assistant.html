Key Concepts Explained
📊 Value-at-Risk (VaR)

VaR answers: "What's the maximum I can expect to lose?" At 95% confidence, there's a 5% chance losses exceed this amount.

📈 Conditional VaR (CVaR)

CVaR answers: "If things go really bad, how bad on average?" It measures the average loss beyond VaR.

⚠️ Basis Risk

The mismatch between the insurance index (rainfall at a station) and actual farm conditions.

💰 Smart Subsidy

Tiered premium support: 25% for low-risk regions, up to 70% for high-risk, low-income regions.

🔬 Extreme Value Theory (EVT)

Statistical method for estimating rare, extreme events not seen in historical data.
