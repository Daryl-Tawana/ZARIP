Reference Concepts
Value-at-Risk (VaR)

The loss threshold exceeded only in the selected tail probability. VaR 95% is a 1-in-20-year planning threshold.

Conditional VaR (CVaR)

The average loss once the VaR threshold is breached. It is the stronger solvency metric for tail-heavy drought risk.

Basis Risk

The mismatch between index-triggered payouts and actual farm losses. It must be disclosed for trust and consumer protection.

Smart Subsidy

A tiered State premium contribution that targets higher support to vulnerable regions while preserving farmer co-payment.
